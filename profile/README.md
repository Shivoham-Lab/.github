## What is Shivoham Lab?

Shivoham (शिवोऽहम्) means *"I am Shiva"*  that is "I am Pure consciousness". The name is intentional. This lab is an extension of who I am and not just another side project I will abandon in three months.

We are an independent research lab exploring **practical AI**, **systems engineering**, and **developer tools**  with an open source default. We build in public, ship early and keep improving. Research papers are a goal but the primary focus is shipping real and useful things.
